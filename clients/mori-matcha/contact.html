Questions we hear a lot

Useful answers before you visit.

Can I come in without knowing anything about matcha?

Yes. Most people do. The shelf and tasting flow are built for first-time buyers.

Do I need a whisk right away?

If you want straight bowls, yes. If you only want lattes, you can start with a shaker and upgrade later.

Can you help with gifts?

Yes. The Stone Whisk Kit and Dawn Ceremonial pair well for first-time gift sets.
