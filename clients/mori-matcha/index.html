Why Mori works

Small shelf. Strong point of view.

Mori Matcha is designed for people who want fewer decisions and better cups. Everything on the shelf earns its place: one product for daily whisking, one for gifting, one for getting started, one for late afternoons.

The idea is simple: if the products make sense together, the customer can move fast and still feel looked after.

1

Sift first

Two grams through a fine sifter keeps the bowl smooth and removes clumps before they start.

2

Whisk short

Fifteen seconds in a tight M motion is enough for a glossy top without turning the drink thin.

3

Pair lightly

Something small and citrus-led helps first-time buyers read matcha as bright, not bitter.
